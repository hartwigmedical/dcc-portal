About Us

The Hartwig Medical Foundation is a not-for-profit organisation with a mission to support research to advance individualized cancer treatment.

A full description of the cohort presented in this portal and underlying methods is available on BioRxiv.

For access to full datasets and controlled tier data, please read these instructions.
